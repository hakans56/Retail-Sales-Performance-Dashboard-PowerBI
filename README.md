# 📊 Retail Sales Performance Dashboard – Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance using an interactive Power BI dashboard.
The goal is to identify key sales trends, customer segments, and regional performance to support business decision-making.

The analysis focuses on sales growth, customer behavior, and product performance across multiple dimensions.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions

The dashboard aims to answer the following questions:

* How are sales trending over time?
* Which regions generate the highest revenue?
* Which product categories drive the most sales?
* Who are the most valuable customer segments?
* What are the top-performing products?

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains retail transaction data including:

* Order information (Order ID, Order Date, Ship Date)
* Customer details (Customer ID, Customer Name, Segment)
* Geographic data (Country, City, State, Region)
* Product information (Category, Sub-Category, Product Name)
* Sales values

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

The dashboard tracks several important KPIs:

* **Total Sales:** $2.26M
* **Total Orders:** 5K
* **Total Customers:** 793
* **Average Order Value:** $459.5
* **Year-over-Year Growth:** 2.77%

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following visual analyses:

### Sales Trend Analysis

* Total Sales vs Previous Year Sales comparison
* Monthly sales performance visualization

### Product Performance

* Total Sales by Category
* Top 10 Products by Sales

### Geographic Insights

* Sales distribution by Region

### Customer Insights

* Sales by Customer Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* **Technology** is the highest revenue-generating category.
* **Consumer segment** drives the largest share of total sales.
* **West and East regions** lead overall revenue performance.
* Sales show **steady growth from 2016 to 2018**.
* Several products consistently contribute a large share of revenue.
